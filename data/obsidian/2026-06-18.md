> [!summary] TL;DR

## 🌰 Prostate

A diagnostic-pathway question, not a therapy result: can PSMA-PET front-run systematic biopsy in biopsy-naive men.

### PRIMARY2 · [[NCT05154162]]

> csPCa detection non-inferior with PSMA-PET vs systematic biopsy (12% vs 16%, diff −3.7%, 95% CI −8.9 to 1.5), and biopsy avoided in 49%.

- 📊 Co-primary 1 (csPCa detection): 12% (39/331) PSMA-PET vs 16% (51/329) systematic biopsy, non-inferior
- 📐 Risk difference −3.7% (95% CI −8.9 to 1.5), p=0.0093; non-inferiority margin 10%
- 📊 Co-primary 2: PSMA-PET avoided biopsy in 49% (163/331), 95% CI 44-55%, p<0.0001 (vs 20% threshold)
- 🔍 Multicentre phase 3 non-inferiority RCT, 7 Australian sites, N=660, 1:1, ITT, no masking
- 🔍 Biopsy-naive men, high clinical risk, PI-RADS 2-3 MRI, PSA ≤20, ≤cT2
- 📊 Post-biopsy morbidity, PSMA-PET vs systematic biopsy (of those biopsied)

  | Symptom | PSMA-PET | Systematic biopsy |
  |---|---|---|
  | Pain | 33 (21%) | 62 (21%) |
  | Haematuria | 60 (38%) | 126 (43%) |
  | Haematospermia | 77 (48%) | 133 (45%) |

- 🔗 Extends PRIMARY (Emmett, 2024): PSMA-PET+MRI improved detection in equivocal/negative MRI; PRIMARY2 is the phase 3 non-inferiority test
- ⚠️ Single country (Australia) and one radiopharmaceutical ([68Ga]Ga-PSMA-11); no masking
- ⚠️ Follow-up ongoing; biopsy-avoided men not fully tracked, so long-term missed-csPCa rate unknown
