# 2026-05-29

**Wait or Treat ([[NCT05236946]]): delayed brain RT → 2yr OS 60% vs 48% (HR 1.45) in EGFR/ALK+ NSCLC aBM; upfront RT halves icPD but OS favors delay.**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Single-day thoracic data from the first completed phase III on RT timing in EGFR/ALK+ NSCLC aBM delivers a discordant result: upfront RT controls intracranial disease but delayed RT outperforms on 2yr OS (60% vs 48%). Adds to the case for TKI-first, RT-at-progression sequencing.

## 🫁 Thoracic / Lung

First phase III data on RT timing in oncogene-driven NSCLC aBM; OS favors delay despite better intracranial control with upfront RT.

### Wait or Treat — Brain RT Timing in EGFR/ALK+ NSCLC (NCT05236946) · [[NCT05236946]]

> Upfront RT halves icPD (sub-HR 0.35, p<0.001) but 2yr OS favors delayed strategy: 60% vs 48%, HR 1.45.

![Wait or Treat — Brain RT Timing in EGFR/ALK+ NSCLC (NCT05236946)](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HJgfW26W0AwCl2W.jpg)

![Wait or Treat — Brain RT Timing in EGFR/ALK+ NSCLC (NCT05236946)](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HJgfW24WMAgfDlr.jpg)

- 📊 OS: 2yr OS 60% (delayed) vs 48% (upfront), HR 1.45 favoring delayed; CI not reported in source
- ⚠️ Radiation necrosis: 6% upfront vs 0% delayed
- 🔍 Both arms: TKIs + chemotherapy; delayed RT delivered at icPD or pt request
- 🔍 MRI surveillance q3m yr1, then q6m
- 🔍 Asymptomatic BM only; ECOG 0-2; stratified by GPA and synchronous vs metachronous BM
- 🔗 First completed phase III RCT for RT timing in oncogene-mutated NSCLC aBM
- ⚠️ Discordant endpoints: icPD reduction with upfront RT does not translate to OS benefit
- ⚠️ Open label; salvage RT rates in delayed arm not reported in source, confounds OS interpretation
