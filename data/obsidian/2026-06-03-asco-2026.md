# 2026-06-03 — ASCO Annual Meeting 2026

**AREST: adjuvant RT lifts 3yr LRFS to 89.2% vs 80.9% (HR 0.52) in intermediate-risk resected oral SCC; LC gain only, no OS/DFS benefit at 47mo.**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Head-neck carried the day: AREST, a phase III RCT (N=392), shows adjuvant RT after resection of intermediate-risk pT1-2N0 oral SCC cuts loco-regional recurrence (3yr LRFS 89.2% vs 80.9%, HR 0.52) but doesn't move OS or DFS at 47mo. Reopens the add-vs-omit RT question in early oral cavity; dose/fractionation unreported.
