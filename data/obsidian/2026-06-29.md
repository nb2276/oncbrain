# 2026-06-29

**BART: adjuvant pelvic RT post-cystectomy cuts 2yr locoregional failure (LRFFS 87.1% vs 76.0%, HR 0.43, p=0.04); no OS benefit (HR 0.78, ns).**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Bladder carried the day. BART, the first phase III of adjuvant pelvic RT after radical cystectomy, roughly halves locoregional failure (8% vs 26%) in high-risk MIBC, benefit concentrating in N+/T3+ pts. OS unchanged, and the trial predates adjuvant nivolumab, so the RT+IO question stays open.

## 💧 Bladder

First randomised evidence for adjuvant RT after radical cystectomy, though underpowered and analyzed per-protocol.

### BART

> 2yr locoregional FFS 87.1% vs 76.0% with adjuvant RT post-cystectomy, HR 0.43 (0.20-0.96), p=0.04; OS not improved (HR 0.78, ns).

> [!info] Why it matters · Radiation oncology
> Offer adjuvant pelvic RT (50.4Gy/28fx, cystectomy bed plus iliac/presacral/obturator nodes) to ≥pT3, N+, or margin-positive pts, where benefit concentrates (N+ HR 0.22) and isolated locoregional failure was eliminated. Late grade 3+ toxicity matched observation (8.4% vs 10.5%, p=0.60), answering the late-morbidity fear. Read it as local control: OS was unchanged.

- 📊 2yr outcomes, adjuvant RT vs observation (LRFFS primary, rest secondary)

  | Endpoint (2yr) | RT | Obs | HR (95% CI) | p |
  |---|---|---|---|---|
  | LRFFS | 87.1% | 76.0% | 0.43 (0.20-0.96) | 0.04 |
  | DFS | 71.6% | 58.7% | 0.62 (0.36-1.05) | 0.07 |
  | BCSS | 79.6% | 65.0% | 0.59 (0.33-1.10) | 0.09 |
  | OS | 70.4% | 57.4% | 0.78 (0.49-1.26) | 0.31 |

- 📊 LR recurrence 8% (RT) vs 26% (obs), p=0.006; no isolated locoregional recurrences with RT
- 🔍 Phase III RCT, N=153 (RT 77 / obs 76), high-risk (T3-4, N1-3, R+) MIBC post-cystectomy + chemo, 2016-2024
- 🔍 RT: cystectomy bed + pelvic nodes (common/internal/external iliac, presacral, obturator), 50.4Gy/28fx, stoma/bowel-sparing IMRT, daily IGRT
- 📊 2yr LRFFS benefit concentrates in high-risk subsets (and per-protocol)

  | 2yr LRFFS analysis/subgroup | HR (95% CI) |
  |---|---|
  | Per-protocol | 0.27 (0.10-0.71), p=0.008 |
  | T3+ and N+ | 0.25 (0.07-0.84) |
  | N+ disease | 0.22 (0.06-0.75) |

- 📊 Toxicity, RT vs observation

  | AE | RT | Obs | p |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | Grade 3 GI | 1.6% | 4.1% | n/a |
  | Grade 2 GI | 17.5% | 1.4% | n/a |
  | Late grade 3+ | 8.4% | 10.5% | 0.60 |

- ⚠️ Primary analysis per-protocol, not ITT: 14 RT-assigned pts (refused 8, progression 4, unfeasible 2) analyzed in observation arm
- ⚠️ Underpowered: trial missed its accrual target
- ⚠️ No immunotherapy given; predates adjuvant nivolumab as SOC, so doesn't address the RT+IO question

> [!question] Open questions
> - OS benefit unconfirmed pending MERCY individual-patient-data meta-analysis
> - Adjuvant RT plus immunotherapy combinations with non-overlapping toxicity
> - Generalizability without immunotherapy and in younger cohort (median age 57)
