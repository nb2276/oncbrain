# 2026-06-29

**BART: adjuvant pelvic RT post-cystectomy cuts 2y locoregional recurrence to 8% vs 26% (LRFFS HR 0.43, p=0.04); OS unchanged (HR 0.78).**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Bladder carried the day: BART is the first RCT of adjuvant pelvic RT after radical cystectomy, roughly halving locoregional recurrence (8% vs 26%, p=0.006) in high-risk MIBC. The LRFFS surrogate hit but OS was null (HR 0.78, p=0.31), and no pt got adjuvant nivolumab, leaving applicability to current IO pathways open.

## 💧 Bladder

First randomised evidence for adjuvant pelvic RT post-cystectomy in high-risk MIBC, a locoregional-control win decided before the adjuvant-IO era.

### BART

> 2y locoregional FFS 87.1% vs 76.0% (HR 0.43, p=0.04) with adjuvant pelvic RT post-cystectomy; OS not significant (HR 0.78, p=0.31).

> [!info] Why it matters · Radiation oncology
> The LRFFS benefit concentrates in the pN+ and T3+ subgroups (2y HR 0.22 and 0.25), and per-protocol it widens to HR 0.27 (93.2% vs 75.0%) once the 14 who never received RT are analysed as observation. Standard 50.4Gy/28fx to bed plus pelvic nodes transfers directly, so this moves the adjuvant-pelvic-RT decision for node-positive or margin-positive disease.

- 🔍 Phase III multicentre RCT, N=153, high-risk MIBC post-radical cystectomy + chemo, randomised to adjuvant RT vs observation
- 🔍 RT: 50.4Gy/28fx to cystectomy bed + pelvic nodes (common/int/ext iliac, presacral, obturator), stoma/bowel-sparing IMRT, daily IGRT
- 📊 2-year outcomes, adjuvant RT vs observation

  | Endpoint (2y) | Adjuvant RT | Observation | HR (95% CI), p |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | LRFFS (ITT) | 87.1% | 76.0% | 0.43 (0.20-0.96), p=0.04 |
  | LRFFS (per-protocol) | 93.2% | 75.0% | 0.27 (0.10-0.71), p=0.008 |
  | DFS | 71.6% | 58.7% | 0.62 (0.36-1.05), p=0.07 |
  | BCSS | 79.6% | 65.0% | 0.59 (0.33-1.10), p=0.09 |
  | OS | 70.4% | 57.4% | 0.78 (0.49-1.26), p=0.31 |

- 📊 Locoregional recurrence 8% (RT) vs 26% (obs), p=0.006; no isolated locoregional recurrences in the RT arm
- 📊 2y LRFFS benefit by subgroup (HR vs observation)

  | Subgroup | HR (95% CI) |
  |---|---|
  | T3+ and N+ | 0.25 (0.07-0.84) |
  | N+ disease | 0.22 (0.06-0.75) |

- ⚠️ Toxicity by arm

  | Adverse event | Adjuvant RT | Observation |
  |---|---|---|
  | Late G3+ | 8.4% | 10.5% (p=0.60) |
  | Acute G3 GI | 1.6% | 4.1% |
  | Acute G2 GI | 17.5% | 1.4% |

- 🔗 Historic locoregional recurrence ~30% post-cystectomy in high-risk pts (≥pT3, N+, <10 nodes, LVI+, margin+); chemo little help locally
- ⚠️ OS not significant (HR 0.78, p=0.31); the 1° endpoint was a locoregional surrogate, not OS
- ⚠️ 14/77 RT-arm pts never received RT (refused/progressed/infeasible), analysed as observation; ITT HR 0.43 vs per-protocol HR 0.27
- ⚠️ No immunotherapy given; adjuvant nivolumab (CheckMate-274) now standard, limiting applicability to current pathways
