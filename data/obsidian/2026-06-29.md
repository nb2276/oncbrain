# 2026-06-29

**BART: adjuvant pelvic RT post-cystectomy cuts 2yr locoregional failure (LRFFS 87.1% vs 76.0%, HR 0.43, p=0.04); OS 70.4 vs 57.4% ns.**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Bladder carried the day: BART, the first RCT of adjuvant pelvic RT after cystectomy, halves locoregional failure (HR 0.43), with benefit concentrated in T3+/N+ disease and no isolated locoregional recurrences in the RT arm. OS ns, underpowered, and predates adjuvant nivolumab.

## 💧 Bladder

### BART

> 2yr locoregional FFS 87.1% vs 76.0% (HR 0.43, p=0.04) with adjuvant pelvic RT after cystectomy; OS 70.4 vs 57.4% ns.

> [!info] Why it matters · Radiation oncology
> The RT read is in the per-protocol and node-positive subgroups: PP 2yr LRFFS 93.2% vs 75.0% (HR 0.27), N+ HR 0.22 (0.06-0.75). Benefit concentrates in N+/T3+ disease. Standard 50.4Gy/28fx IMRT with no excess late G3+ toxicity (8.4 vs 10.5%), so this moves the adjuvant-RT decision for N+ MIBC.

- 📊 Efficacy by endpoint at 2yr (adjuvant RT vs observation)

  | Endpoint (2yr) | Adj RT | Obs | HR (95% CI), p |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | LRFFS (ITT) | 87.1% | 76.0% | 0.43 (0.20-0.96), p=0.04 |
  | LRFFS (per-protocol) | 93.2% | 75.0% | 0.27 (0.10-0.71), p=0.008 |
  | DFS | 71.6% | 58.7% | 0.62 (0.36-1.05), p=0.07 |
  | BCSS | 79.6% | 65.0% | 0.59 (0.33-1.10), p=0.09 |
  | OS | 70.4% | 57.4% | 0.78 (0.49-1.26), p=0.31 |

- 🔍 Phase III multicenter RCT, N=153 (RT 77, obs 76), accrued 2016-2024, 1:1
- 📐 Adjuvant RT 50.4Gy/28fx to cystectomy bed + pelvic nodes, stoma-sparing IMRT, daily IGRT
- 📊 LRFFS benefit concentrates in node-positive / T3+ (HR, RT vs obs)

  | Subgroup | HR (95% CI) |
  |---|---|
  | T3+ and N+ | 0.25 (0.07-0.84) |
  | N+ disease | 0.22 (0.06-0.75) |

- 📊 Locoregional recurrence 8% (RT) vs 26% (obs), p=0.006
- 📊 No isolated locoregional recurrences in the RT arm (pattern of failure)
- ⚠️ Toxicity (RT vs observation)
  - Late G3+ similar: 8.4% vs 10.5%, p=0.60
  - Acute G2 GI higher with RT: 17.5% vs 1.4%, no discontinuation
  - Acute G3 GI: 1.6% vs 4.1%
- ⚠️ 1° EP is locoregional FFS (surrogate); OS ns (HR 0.78, p=0.31), trial underpowered
- ⚠️ No immunotherapy given; predates adjuvant nivolumab SOC, RT+IO combos untested here
