## 🌰 Prostate

Oligomet prostate MDT evidence is maturing across multiple RCTs, with ARTO providing the first randomised OS signal in the castration-resistant setting.

### EAU 2026: MDT/SBRT Intensification in Oligometastatic Prostate Cancer (Fonteyne)

> EAU review: MDT/SBRT delays progression and defers systemic Tx in oligomet HSPC; ARTO is the first RCT hinting at an OS benefit.

- 📊 Most MDT evidence is metachronous oligorecurrent HSPC: delays progression, defers systemic therapy
- 📊 ARTO: first randomized signal MDT may improve OS + PCSS (added to SOC, castration-resistant oligomets)
- 📊 WOLVERINE: MDT improved PFS + rPFS and delayed progression to CRPC, but OS not significant
- 🔗 Other MDT trials cited (directional, no effect sizes in source)
  - RADIOSA: ADT + MDT improved PFS vs MDT alone
  - STOMP / ORIOLE: excellent local control, delayed progression, minimal toxicity
  - RAVENS: adding Ra-223 to MDT did not improve PFS or MFS
  - LUNAR (ongoing): Lu-177 + MDT; radioligand + MDT not yet shown to add benefit
- 🔍 De novo oligomet evidence thin; ongoing STAMPEDE2 (SBRT), PLATON, TERPS, OLIGOPRESTO
- 🔍 SOLAR (n=21): testosterone recovery with sustained low PSA after MDT, a treatment-free-interval concept
- ⚠️ SOLAR (synchronous) vs SATURN (metachronous): synchronous appeared more favorable, hypothesis-generating only
- ⚠️ Trials small with heterogeneous inclusion + endpoints, so guideline integration stays unresolved
- ⚠️ Selection is the key gap; PSMA PET burden (PP3) may refine beyond the ≤5-lesion cutoff
