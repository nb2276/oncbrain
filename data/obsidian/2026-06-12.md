## 🌰 Prostate

MDT/SBRT evidence is maturing across oligomet prostate states, with ARTO providing the first RCT OS signal, but de novo HSPC and patient selection remain open.

### EAU 2026: MDT/SBRT in oligometastatic prostate cancer (Fonteyne review)

> MDT/SBRT delays progression across oligomet prostate states; only ARTO suggests an OS benefit; de novo HSPC and patient selection remain unsettled.

- 🔍 EAU 2026 thematic-session review (Fonteyne, Ghent radonc) of MDT/SBRT evidence across oligomet prostate states, not a single trial
- 📊 Oligorecurrent HSPC carries most data: STOMP, ORIOLE showed high local control, delayed progression, minimal toxicity (no effect sizes reported in source)
- 📊 RADIOSA: ADT plus MDT improved PFS vs MDT alone (no effect size reported in source)
- 📊 WOLVERINE: MDT added to SOC improved PFS, rPFS and delayed CRPC, but no significant OS benefit (no effect sizes reported in source)
- 🔗 ARTO (castration-resistant oligomet): MDT plus SOC improved PFS, OS and PCSS; per Fonteyne the first RCT signal of an OS benefit (no effect sizes reported in source)
- ⚠️ De novo oligomet HSPC: high-level evidence lacking; awaiting STAMPEDE2 (SBRT), PLATON, TERPS, OLIGOPRESTO
- ⚠️ Radioligand plus MDT unproven: RAVENS (Ra-223 plus MDT) did not improve PFS or MFS vs MDT alone; LUNAR (Lu-177) ongoing
- 🔍 SOLAR (N=21, synchronous): tested ADT discontinuation after MDT, tracking testosterone recovery with sustained low PSA; outcomes favored synchronous vs SATURN (metachronous), hypothesis-generating
- ⚠️ Selection gates benefit, not lesion count alone: PSMA-PET burden (PP3 retrospective) may refine OS stratification; systemic therapy alone remains standard for most pts
