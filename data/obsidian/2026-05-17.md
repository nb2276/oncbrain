# 2026-05-17

**OLIGOMA: mPFS 35.8 vs 20.4mo, HR 0.48 (p=0.021); first positive breast-specific MDT RCT despite early stop at N=87.**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Breast carried the meeting: OLIGOMA (HR 0.48, first positive MDT RCT for oligoMBC) plus 10yr confirmations from DBCG HYPO, IMPORT HIGH, and EORTC 22922 closing longstanding boost/IM-MS questions. Prostate was a null day. PEACE-2 interim shows no pelvic RT benefit (cPFS HR 0.81, p=0.088); efficacy endpoints still pending across PACE-NODES, PIVOTALboost, and PRIME.

## 🌰 Prostate

A high-volume pelvic RT data day that mostly raised questions: PEACE-2 interim is the headline null, with three other efficacy endpoints still pending.

### PACE-NODES

> PACE-NODES: pelvic nodal SBRT increases acute GI toxicity (28% vs 21% G2+) but resolves by 12 weeks; GU toxicity equivalent.

- 🔍 Phase 3 RCT, N=1166, high-risk localised prostate (cT3a-T4 and/or Gleason 8-10 and/or PSA>20), 12-36mo ADT; 1:1 P-SBRT (36.25Gy/5f prostate) vs PPN-SBRT (36.25Gy/5f prostate + 25Gy/5f nodes) on alternate days
- 📊 Primary acute toxicity EPs: CTCAE G2+ GI 28% (PPN-SBRT) vs 21% (P-SBRT) over 12 weeks; no difference in GU toxicity between arms
- ⚠️ 11% PPN-SBRT vs 4% P-SBRT did not receive allocation, mostly dose-constraint failures; per-protocol population diverges meaningfully from ITT
- 🔗 GI signal consistent with POP-RT and PEACE-2 pelvic nodal RT data; toxicity magnitude comparable but SBRT fractionation is novel in this context
- ❓ Primary EP (time to biochemical or clinical failure) not yet mature; this report is toxicity-only; cancer control data pending

### RADIOSA

> RADIOSA post-hoc: SBRT+ADT vs SBRT alone — median MFS 16.6mo vs NR, HR 0.39 (p=0.001) in oligorecurrent prostate.

![RADIOSA](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIdDCQkW0AAvnIw.jpg)

- 📊 Metastatic progression: 32/51 (62.7%) Arm A vs 19/51 (37.3%) Arm B; median MFS 16.6mo (95% CI 12.83-NR) vs NR; HR 0.39 (95% CI 0.22-0.69), p=0.001
- 🔍 Phase II RCT, N=102, 1:1 SBRT alone vs SBRT + 6mo ADT; oligorecurrent prostate cancer; median f/u 49.2mo (95% CI 42.5-54.8)
- 📊 Eugonadal MFS (post-testosterone recovery) also favored Arm B, p<0.05; benefit persisted beyond pharmacologic castration
- ⚠️ Post-hoc analysis of a phase II trial; MFS as primary metric here is a surrogate endpoint not prespecified as primary; small N (51/arm) limits power for subgroup inference
- 🔗 Aligns with ORIOLE/STOMP signal of metastasis-directed therapy + systemic intensification in oligorecurrent disease; ADT addition consistent with emerging combination approach
- ❓ Whether eugonadal MFS translates to OS benefit remains unestablished; optimal ADT duration and pt selection for combination vs SBRT alone unclear

### PIVOTALboost

> PIVOTALboost phase 3: focal boost + pelvic nodal RT (20 fx), no increase in 2-yr late G2+ bowel/bladder toxicity.

![PIVOTALboost](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIiVWznXoAAqNwC.jpg)

|---|---|---|

| Prostate+Nodes+Boost (n=347) | 6.5% (4.5-9.5) | 16.5% (13.1-20.6) |

### PEACE-2

> PEACE-2 (interim): pelvic RT added no significant cPFS benefit over prostate-only RT in very high-risk PCa (HR 0.81, p=0.088).

![PEACE-2](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhXl7VWkAAwqHi.jpg)

| Arm | 7-yr cPFS | HR (95% CI) | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prostate-only RT | 62.9% [57.4; 68.1] | ref | — |
| Pelvic RT | 67.1% [61.6; 72.2] | 0.81 [0.63; 1.03] | 0.088 |

- 🔍 Phase III RCT, N=761; ADT 3yr; dose-escalated 78 Gy EQD2; very high-risk N0M0 (≥2 of: Gleason ≥8/T3-T4/PSA ≥20); PSMA PET/CT staging
- 🔗 Whole-pelvis RT vs prostate-only: PEACE-2 vs POP-RT across endpoints (HR <1 favors WPRT)

  | Endpoint | POP-RT HR (95% CI), p | PEACE-2 HR (95% CI), p |
  |---|---|---|
  | bFFS/bPFS | 0.50 (0.42-0.61), <0.001 | 0.97 (0.81-1.16), 0.73 |
  | cFFS/cPFS | 0.74 (0.61-0.90), 0.002 | 0.81 (0.63-1.03), 0.09 |
  | MFS | 0.72 (0.58-0.89), 0.002 | 0.93 (0.74-1.17), 0.54 |

- ⚠️ Interim analysis (~5.5yr f/u); prespecified final pending. Era mismatch vs POP-RT: PSMA staging + longer ADT likely elevates control-arm baseline efficacy.
- ⚠️ <10% PCa-specific mortality at 10yr; author challenges whether this cohort truly qualifies as 'very high-risk' in PSMA PET era.
- ❓ Final OS data awaited; optimal WPRT candidate selection in PSMA-staged localized PCa unresolved.

### PRIME · [[NCT03561961]]

> PRIME interim: 5-fx SBRT vs 25-fx moderate hypo with pelvic RT + ADT in high-risk/node+ prostate; no early inferiority signal, grade 3+ tox <1%.

- 🔍 Phase III non-inferiority, N~434; high-risk/very high-risk/node+ non-metastatic prostate; accrual 2018-2023
  - Arm A: 36.25 Gy/5fx SBRT (7.25 Gy/fx, every other day)
  - Arm B: ~68 Gy/25fx moderate hypo (2.7 Gy/fx, 5fx/week, ~5wk)
  - Both arms: whole-pelvis nodal RT 25 Gy/5fx + long-course ADT ~2yr
- 📊 1° EP BFFS (Phoenix, interim 1-2yr f/u): no inferiority signal for SBRT arm; no HR or p-value reported in source; 4-5yr mature data awaited
- 📊 Acute toxicity (≤90 days)

  | Endpoint | SBRT (5fx) | Moderate Hypo (25fx) |
  |---|---|---|
  | GU ≥G2 | ~5.4% | ~4.0% |
  | GI ≥G2 | ~2.2% | ~3.7% |
  | G3+ GU/GI | <1% | <1% |

- 📊 Late toxicity (up to 1-2yr)

  | Endpoint | SBRT (5fx) | Moderate Hypo (25fx) | p |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | GU ≥G2 | ~10-12% | ~9-11% | 0.59 |
  | GI ≥G2 | ~5-7% | ~4-6% | 0.20 |
  | G3+ GU/GI | <1% | <1% | NS |

- 🔗 vs HYPO-RT-PC (10yr FFS, adjHR 0.84, 95% CI 0.69-1.03): non-inferiority confirmed — but node-negative only, no ADT, no pelvic RT; different population
- ⚠️ Interim analysis; 1-2yr f/u insufficient for BFFS conclusions in high-risk/node+ disease; non-inferiority not yet formally established

## 🎀 Breast

Long-term data day: 10yr to 20yr follow-up from five trials converge on hypofractionation maturity and IM-MS limits, alongside the first positive breast MDT RCT.

### HypoG-01

> HypoG-01 patterns-of-failure analysis: LRR infrequent (20/118 events), comparable between 40 Gy/15 fx and 50 Gy/25 fx arms.

![118 first oncological events: iLRR 16%, second malignancy 31%, IDR 51%; LRR 20/118 events. LRR sites: 20/30 in-volume (67%), 19/30 nodal.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIdG7e-XAAA0w5K.jpg)
*118 first oncological events: iLRR 16%, second malignancy 31%, IDR 51%; LRR 20/118 events. LRR sites: 20/30 in-volume (67%), 19/30 nodal.*

- 🔍 Pre-planned secondary analysis of HypoG-01 phase III (ITT); N=1,260, median f/u 4.8 yrs; randomised 3-wk (40 Gy/15 fx) vs 5-wk (50 Gy/25 fx) RT + tumour-bed boost
- 📊 118 first oncological events: isolated DR 51%, second malignancy 31%, iLRR 16%; LRR lowest-frequency event category
- 📊 Of 30 LRR sites: 20/30 (67%) in-volume, 19/30 nodal (mainly L1 & L2)
- 🔗 Failure pattern not obviously different between hypofractionation arms; supports equivalence of 40 Gy/15 fx to 50 Gy/25 fx for locoregional control
- ⚠️ No effect sizes or HRs reported for LRR/DR by arm; this is a descriptive patterns-of-failure analysis, not a powered efficacy comparison between arms
- ❓ Whether dosimetric margins per ESTRO guidelines need adjustment for nodal L1/L2 coverage remains open

### OLIGOMA · [[NCT04495309]]

> OLIGOMA: mPFS 35.8 vs 20.4mo, HR 0.48 (p=0.021) favoring ablative RT + systemic therapy in oligoMBC; first positive RCT for MDT.

![OLIGOMA](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhYrEZWgAI2Ndq.jpg)

- 📊 QoL co-primary: EORTC QLQ-C30 72.2 vs 74.3 (ANCOVA diff -2.1, 95% CI -9.2 to 5.1); non-inferiority margin -10pts met
- 🔍 RCT, N=87, oligoMBC ≤5 mets, any treatment line; LART to all lesions + systemic vs systemic alone; stratified by systemic type and line
- 📐 ~77% ER+, HER2-neg dominant; >80% had 1-3 mets; ~2/3 lesions bone; ~72% first-line systemic at enrollment
- ⚠️ Stopped early: <20% of initial enrollment target, <40% of amended target; wide HR CI 0.25-0.91 reflects N=87; OS not reported in source
- 🔗 First breast-specific randomized MDT trial; prior PFS signals from SABR-COMET (mixed histologies) and ORIOLE (predominantly prostate)
- ❓ Which pts benefit most (bone-dominant vs visceral, 1-3 vs 4-5 mets, line of therapy)? TAORMINA, STEREO-SEIN, LARA, CLEAR ongoing

### DBCG HYPO

> DBCG HYPO 10yr: 40 Gy/15fr cut G2-3 breast induration 19.5% vs 24.7% (HR 0.76, p=0.005) with no difference in recurrence or OS.

![DBCG HYPO](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhgdACW0AAaAeV.jpg)

| Endpoint | 50 Gy/25fr | 40 Gy/15fr | HR (95% CI) | p |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 10-yr G2-3 induration | 24.7% | 19.5% | 0.76 (0.62-0.92) | 0.005 |
| 10-yr OS | 92.1% | 93.0% | 0.81 (0.63-1.04) | 0.10 |

- 🔍 Phase III non-inferiority, N=1,882, node-negative breast cancer or DCIS, Denmark/Norway/Germany, 2009-2014; median f/u 12.8yr
- 📊 No significant diff in LRR, distant failure, or breast cancer mortality at 10yr
- 🔗 Consistent with START-B/UK HypoFrac 10-yr data; DBCG HYPO adds Nordic/German confirmation at longer f/u

- ❓ Ultra-hypofractionation (FAST-Forward 26Gy/5fr) entering practice; direct 10-yr toxicity comparison vs DBCG HYPO still pending

|---|---|---|

| 53Gy/15F (3.5Gy/F) | 5.5% | (4.1, 7.3) |

### APBI-IMRT Florence (NCT02104895) · [[NCT02104895]]

> APBI-IMRT Florence 15yr: IBTR 7.7% vs 4.2%, HR 1.57 (0.82-3.04), p=0.17; no OS, locoregional, or DM difference.

![APBI-IMRT Florence (NCT02104895)](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhdbJgW4AAHzXl.jpg)

- 🔍 Phase III equivalence trial, N=520, 1:1 APBI 30Gy/5# vs WBI 50Gy/25#+10Gy boost; BCS, pT<25mm, FSM≥5mm, age>40; enrolled 2005-2013
- 📊 IBTR excess in APBI driven by new ipsilateral primaries (5.9% vs 2.7%, p=0.09), not true local relapse (2.1% vs 1.6%, p=0.75)
- 📊 15yr locoregional 7.2% vs 5.0% (p=0.28), distant mets 2.7% vs 4.6% (p=0.35), deaths 21.5% vs 19.6% (p=0.66), all NS
- 🔗 Local relapse stable from 10-15yr (2.1% APBI vs 1.6% WBI both timepoints); IBTR divergence after 10yr driven entirely by new primaries

## 🎯 Oligometastatic / Mets

### EXTEND

> EXTEND phase II RCT: MDT added to SOC for oligometastatic solid tumors; primary analysis published JCO May 2026.

- 🔍 Phase II randomized basket trial, MDT vs SOC alone across multiple tumor histologies in oligometastatic setting; primary aggregated analysis with all tumor-histology baskets now published
- 📊 No effect sizes reported in source tweets; primary analysis data not extractable from tweet + OCR alone
- 💊 MDT arm incorporated metastasis-directed therapy (likely SBRT/SABR) added to standard of care
- 🔗 ctDNA correlatives presented synchronously at ESTRO26, suggesting biomarker substratification component beyond clinical endpoints
- ⚠️ Phase II basket design: histology-pooled primary analysis may obscure site-specific heterogeneity; basket trials frequently powered for aggregated not per-histology endpoints
- ❓ Per-histology basket breakdown and whether MDT benefit is uniform or histology-dependent is the key unanswered question from this primary analysis
