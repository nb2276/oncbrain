# 2026-05-17

**OLIGOMA: mPFS 35.8 vs 20.4mo (HR 0.48, p=0.021), first RCT to show MDT PFS benefit in oligometastatic breast.**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Prostate dominated: PIVOTALboost, PACE-NODES, and PRIME confirm pelvic nodal RT feasibility at 5-20 fractions; PEACE 2 complicates the picture (pelvic RT null under PSMA staging). Breast carried a sweep of 10-yr confirmatory data. OLIGOMA is the day's efficacy outlier: first RCT showing MDT PFS benefit in oligometastatic breast (HR 0.48, p=0.021).

## 🌰 Prostate

Three trials probe pelvic nodal RT feasibility across fractionation schedules (5 fx to 20 fx); PEACE 2 challenges whether that benefit survives PSMA PET staging.

### PIVOTALboost

> PIVOTALboost: focal boost + pelvic nodal RT (20 fx) safe at 2 yrs; no increase in late G2+ bowel/bladder toxicity vs prostate-only.

![PIVOTALboost](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIiVWznXoAAqNwC.jpg)

| Arm | n | G2+ Bowel (2yr) | G2+ Bladder (2yr) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Prostate | 281 | 8.2% (5.7-11.7) | 19.5% (15.5-24.4) |
| Prostate+Boost | 345 | 8.7% (6.3-12.1) | 24.1% (20.2-28.7) |
| PPN+Boost | 347 | 6.5% (4.5-9.5) | 16.5% (13.1-20.6) |

![PIVOTALboost](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIiVWumXYAAAIXc.jpg)

- 🔍 Phase 3 RCT, N=1465 randomised, 39 UK centres; high-risk localised prostate; 20-fraction IMRT ± focal boost ± pelvic nodal RT (2×2 factorial)
- 📊 Secondary toxicity endpoints reported here; primary EP (biochemical/clinical failure) pending
- 📊 Early bowel SE increased with pelvic nodal RT (PPN+B arm) but resolved by 18 wks post-RT
- 📊 Late G2+ bowel and bladder SE similar across arms at 2 yrs (973 pts, 74% with ≥2-yr f/u)
- 🔗 Consistent with FLAME (Lancet Oncol 2021): focal boost improved bDFS without toxicity increase, now replicated in 20-fraction UK setting
- ⚠️ Primary efficacy endpoint not yet reported; this is toxicity-only data
- ⚠️ 74% with ≥2-yr f/u; longer-term late effects still accruing; G3+ rates not separately quantified in source

### PACE-NODES

> PACE-NODES: pelvic nodal SBRT feasible; acute GI G≥2 28% vs 21% (resolved by 12 wk); GU unchanged. Efficacy data pending.

![PACE-NODES](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhsLTUXoAAbiFs.jpg)

| Outcome | PPN-SBRT | P-SBRT |
|---|---|---|
| CTCAE G≥2 GI (12 wk) | 28% | 21% |

![Phase 3 1:1 RCT schema. P-SBRT: 36.25Gy/5f prostate; PPN-SBRT: 36.25Gy/5f prostate + 25Gy/5f nodes. Target N=1128.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhsLcDXAAAcNgO.jpg)
*Phase 3 1:1 RCT schema. P-SBRT: 36.25Gy/5f prostate; PPN-SBRT: 36.25Gy/5f prostate + 25Gy/5f nodes. Target N=1128.*

- 🔍 Phase 3 RCT; high-risk prostate (T3a-T4, Gleason 8-10, or PSA>20ng/ml) + 12-36mo ADT; N=1166 randomized
- 💊 P-SBRT: 36.25Gy/5f prostate; PPN-SBRT: 36.25Gy/5f prostate + 25Gy/5f pelvic nodes, on alternate days
- 📊 Primary endpoint (time to biochemical or clinical failure): not yet mature. Acute toxicity analysis only.
- 📊 CTCAE G≥2 GI toxicity (12-wk window): 28% PPN-SBRT vs 21% P-SBRT
- 📊 GI symptoms resolved by 12 wk: no between-arm difference at that timepoint
- 📊 CTCAE G≥2 GU toxicity: no difference between arms (clinician-reported and PRO)
- 📊 EPIC-26 bowel domain worse in PPN-SBRT arm at 4 wk (GI PRO signal)
- ⚠️ 11% PPN-SBRT vs 4% P-SBRT did not receive allocated treatment, mostly planning constraints not met
- 🔗 Pelvic nodal RT benefit in high-risk prostate established with moderate hypofractionation (PEACE-2, POP-RT); PACE-NODES extends this to 5-fraction SBRT

- ⚠️ post-hoc analysis: MFS not the prespecified primary endpoint of RADIOSA; hypothesis-generating only

### PEACE 2

> PEACE-2: pelvic RT adds no significant cPFS benefit over prostate-only RT in PSMA-staged very high-risk PCa (HR 0.81, p=0.09).

![PEACE 2](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhXl7VWkAAwqHi.jpg)

| Arm | 7-yr cPFS | HR (95% CI) | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pelvic RT | 67.1% [61.6; 72.2] | 0.81 [0.63; 1.03] | 0.088 |
| Prostate-only RT | 62.9% [57.4; 68.1] | reference | — |

![PEACE 2](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIkfsYnaYAAfa1-.jpg)

| Endpoint | POP-RT HR (95% CI) | POP-RT p | PEACE-2 HR (95% CI) | PEACE-2 p |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| bPFS/bFFS | 0.50 (0.42-0.61) | <0.001 | 0.97 (0.81-1.16) | 0.73 |
| cPFS/cFFS | 0.74 (0.61-0.90) | 0.002 | 0.81 (0.63-1.03) | 0.09 |
| MFS | 0.72 (0.58-0.89) | 0.002 | 0.93 (0.74-1.17) | 0.54 |

![PEACE 2](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhXl7XXMAADF2L.jpg)

- 🔍 Phase III, 4-arm (prostate vs pelvic RT × ± cabazitaxel × 4c); ADT 3y; N0M0, ≥2 of: Gleason ≥8, T3-T4, PSA ≥20
- 📊 1° EP cPFS: HR 0.81 (0.63-1.03), p=0.088; primary endpoint not met
- ⚠️ Staged with PSMA PET/CT (vs conventional CT/bone scan in POP-RT); occult-met exclusion likely attenuates incremental pelvic RT benefit
- ⚠️ Longer ADT (3y vs 2y POP-RT) and higher prostate dose (78 Gy vs 74-76 Gy EQD2) further confound cross-trial comparison
- 🔗 POP-RT (conventional staging, 2y ADT) hit bFFS, cFFS, and MFS significantly; PEACE-2 null on all — cross-trial breakdown in adjacent figure
- ⚠️ Presenter conclusion: <10% PCa mortality at 10y challenges whether pts truly qualify as 'very high-risk' under modern PSMA PET staging
- 🔍 Median f/u ~5.5y; plenary presentation ESTRO 2026

### PRIME · [[NCT03561961]]

> PRIME: G3+ tox <1% both arms; 5-fx SBRT vs mod hypo + whole-pelvis RT + ADT in high-risk/node-positive PCa; BFFS pending.

![PRIME](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIkmdCjaMAAFt57.jpg)

| Toxicity | SBRT 5fx | Mod Hypo 25fx | p |
|---|---|---|---|
| Acute GU G≥2 | ~5.4% | ~4.0% | NS |
| Acute GI G≥2 | ~2.2% | ~3.7% | NS |
| Acute G3+ | <1% | <1% | NS |
| Late GU G≥2 | ~10-12% | ~9-11% | 0.59 |
| Late GI G≥2 | ~5-7% | ~4-6% | 0.20 |
| Late G3+ | <1% | <1% | — |

![HYPO-RT-PC 10-yr FFS: 72% vs 65%; HR 0.84 (95% CI 0.69-1.03), non-inferiority confirmed. PRIME efficacy not yet mature.](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIkmcyeaQAAEf2f.jpg)
*HYPO-RT-PC 10-yr FFS: 72% vs 65%; HR 0.84 (95% CI 0.69-1.03), non-inferiority confirmed. PRIME efficacy not yet mature.*

- 📊 Primary BFFS (interim): no inferiority signal for SBRT arm; no HR/p reported in source; mature 4-5yr data awaited
- 📊 MFS, OS: no difference at interim; data immature; long-term follow-up ongoing
- 🔍 Phase III open-label non-inferiority RCT; N ~434; Tata Memorial Centre + India; accrual 2018-2023
- 🔍 Arm A: SBRT 36.25 Gy/5 fx, every other day; Arm B: ~68 Gy/25 fx, 5 fractions/week
- 🔍 Both arms: whole-pelvis RT 25 Gy/5 fx + long-course ADT ~2 years
- 🔍 Eligibility: high-risk, very high-risk, node-positive non-metastatic PCa; ECOG 0-2; PSMA PET/CT staging allowed
- 💊 QoL (EORTC QLQ-C30/PR25, IPSS): comparable between arms
  - No meaningful deterioration in global health status
  - Transient declines during/post-treatment with recovery over time
  - ADT-related sexual/hormonal effects similar both arms
- 🔗 HYPO-RT-PC (node-negative, no ADT, no pelvic RT): 10-yr FFS non-inferior, HR 0.84; PRIME extends to node-positive + whole-pelvis
- ⚠️ Interim analysis only; 1-2yr follow-up; primary BFFS endpoint not mature
- ⚠️ Single-country academic-center cohort; PSMA-staged population may differ from community practice

## 🎀 Breast

Long-term confirmatory sweep: DBCG HYPO, IMPORT HIGH, and Dutch Breast Boost extend the 10-yr evidence base across fractionation, boost de-escalation, and the modern-systemic-therapy era; OLIGOMA is the outlier positive efficacy result.

### OLIGOMA · [[NCT04495309]]

> OLIGOMA: mPFS 35.8 vs 20.4mo (HR 0.48, p=0.021) favoring MDT in oligometastatic breast; QoL non-inferior.

![OLIGOMA](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhYrEZWgAI2Ndq.jpg)

![QoL at 12wks: EORTC QLQ-C30 mean 72.2 (67.2-77.2) exp vs 74.3 (69.3-79.3) control; difference -2.1 (-9.2-5.1)](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhYrAwXYAA_Wd6.jpg)
*QoL at 12wks: EORTC QLQ-C30 mean 72.2 (67.2-77.2) exp vs 74.3 (69.3-79.3) control; difference -2.1 (-9.2-5.1)*

![OLIGOMA](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhYrEdWoAADclc.jpg)

- 🔍 randomized, N=87, oligometastatic breast ≤5 lesions, any line; stratified by systemic therapy type and line
- 🔍 majority ER+/PR+, HER2-neg; >80% had 1-3 mets; 2/3 of lesions were bone mets
- 🔍 ~73% receiving 1st-line systemic (endocrine or chemo); 27% 2nd-line
- 📊 QoL co-primary (n=64): EORTC QLQ-C30 mean 72.2 (exp) vs 74.3 (control) at 12 wks
- 📐 QoL difference -2.1 (-9.2 to 5.1); non-inferiority met (margin: -10 pts)
- ⚠️ stopped early: <20% of initial planned accrual, <40% of amended target; N=87 total
- ⚠️ wide PFS CI (0.25-0.91) reflects underpowering; OS not reported in source
- 🔗 first RCT to show MDT PFS benefit in oligometastatic breast; TAORMINA, STEREO-SEIN, LARA, OLIGAMI ongoing

### DBCG HYPO

> DBCG HYPO 10yr: 40Gy/15fr vs 50Gy, 10-yr induration 19.5% vs 24.7% (HR 0.76, p=0.005); no recurrence or survival difference.

![DBCG HYPO](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhgdACW0AAaAeV.jpg)

|---|---|---|---|---|

| 10-yr OS | 92.1% | 93.0% | 0.81 (0.63-1.04) | 0.10 |

![DBCG HYPO](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HIhgc-kXUAEmvDg.jpg)

- 🔍 Phase III RCT 1:1, N=1,882, node-negative breast or DCIS, Denmark/Norway/Germany, 2009-2014
- 🔍 Whole-breast RT 50Gy/25fr vs 40Gy/15fr; median f/u 12.8 yr
- 🔍 1° EP was 3-yr grade ≥2 induration; these are prespecified 10-yr analyses
