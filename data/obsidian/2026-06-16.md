# 2026-06-16

**REVELUTION: +68.9 mm³ more coronary plaque at 12mo with leuprolide vs relugolix in men on ADT + pelvic RT · relugolix may spare the heart.**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Single prostate signal today: in men on ≥6mo ADT with definitive pelvic RT, relugolix produced less coronary plaque progression than leuprolide (adjusted 68.9 mm³ difference, NCPV-driven), a mechanistic correlate for HERO's MACE benefit. Surrogate imaging endpoint, N=94, 12-mo f/u · hypothesis-generating for ADT-agent choice when RT mandates androgen suppression.

## 🌰 Prostate

Cardiovascular safety, not oncologic efficacy: the day's only prostate readout compares two ADT agents' coronary impact in men already committed to pelvic RT.

### REVELUTION

> Adjusted 12-mo coronary plaque volume rose 68.9 mm³ more with leuprolide vs relugolix in men on ADT with pelvic RT.

- 📊 1° EP: 12-mo change in total coronary plaque volume (serial CCTA, HeartFlow automated quantification)
- 📊 Crude 12-mo total plaque change: +56 mm³ leuprolide vs +25 mm³ relugolix
- 📐 Adjusted between-arm difference 68.9 mm³ more total plaque with leuprolide (ANCOVA: age, statin use, baseline plaque)
- 📊 Plaque-subtype breakdown
  - Total-plaque difference driven mainly by non-calcified plaque (NCPV)
  - Calcified plaque: no significant between-arm difference
  - Low-attenuation plaque: no significant between-arm difference, low absolute change
- 🔍 Single-institution open-label RCT, 94 men, intermediate/high-risk non-metastatic prostate ca (June 2020-2024)
- 🔍 ADT pts randomized 1:1: relugolix (360mg load → 120mg/d) vs leuprolide 3-mo depot, ≥6mo ADT
- 🔍 All pts got pelvic RT ± nodes; ADT agent was the only randomized variable (CCTA reads blinded, stratified by ASCVD 10-yr risk)
- 🔗 Mechanistic correlate for HERO (2020), which showed lower MACE with relugolix vs leuprolide
- ⚠️ Limitations
  - Surrogate imaging endpoint (plaque volume), not clinical MACE
  - 12-mo follow-up; single institution; N=94
  - No p-values or CIs for the total-plaque difference reported in source
  - RT-alone (no-ADT) control arm was a parallel non-randomized prospective cohort
