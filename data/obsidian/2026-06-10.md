**NRG/RTOG 0848: adjuvant chemoRT adds no OS overall post-pancreatic head resection (HR 0.96, p=0.38); node-negative subgroup gains 5-yr OS 48% vs 29% (interaction p=0.022).**

## 🟡 Hepatobiliary

A clean randomized test of adjuvant chemoRT after pancreatic head resection comes up empty overall; RT moved the needle only in the node-negative subgroup.

### NRG Oncology/RTOG 0848 · [[NCT01013649]]

> OS HR 0.96 (90% CI 0.79-1.18), p=0.38: adjuvant chemoRT adds no OS benefit over chemo alone after pancreatic head resection.

> [!info] Why it matters · Radiation oncology
> The RT read is the node-negative subgroup: 5-yr OS 28.6→48.1% and median OS 3.0→3.9 yr with added CXRT (interaction p=0.022), while node-positive pts gained nothing (21.2 vs 20.9%). Subgroup-only (n=91), but it isolates who might still warrant adjuvant CXRT once modern systemic therapy is optimized.

- 🔍 RT randomization of NRG/RTOG 0848, N=354 post-resection + adjuvant gemcitabine chemo: no further tx (n=174) vs fluoropyrimidine-sensitized CXRT (n=180)
- 📊 1° EP not met: adjuvant CXRT adds no OS benefit overall
- 📐 OS HR 0.96 (90% CI 0.79-1.18), p=0.38 (1-sided); median OS 2.6 vs 2.3 yr
- 📊 5-yr OS by nodal status (chemo vs chemo+CXRT), interaction p=0.022

  | Nodal status | Chemo 5-yr OS | Chemo+CXRT 5-yr OS |
  |---|---|---|
  | N0 (n=91) | 28.6% (14.9-42.2) | 48.1% (33.3-62.9) |
  | N1 (n=263) | 21.2% (13.8-28.5) | 20.9% (13.8-28.0) |

- 📊 Node-negative (N0) subgroup medians, chemo vs chemo+CXRT

  | Endpoint | Chemo | Chemo+CXRT |
  |---|---|---|
  | Median OS | 3.0 yr (2.2-4.0) | 3.9 yr (2.5-NR) |
  | Median DFS | 1.5 yr (0.8-2.7) | 2.3 yr (1.4-NR) |

- ⚠️ Node-negative OS gain is a subgroup finding (n=91, interaction p=0.022); hypothesis-generating, needs independent confirmation
- ⚠️ RT dose, fractionation, and target volume not reported in source text
- 🔗 Gemcitabine backbone (gem alone n=236, gem+erlotinib n=100) predates FOLFIRINOX-era adjuvant standard
