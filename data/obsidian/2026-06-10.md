# 2026-06-10

**NRG/RTOG 0848: adjuvant chemoRT misses OS overall (HR 0.96, p=0.38), but node-negative subgroup 5-yr OS doubles 28.6%→48.1% (interaction p=0.022).**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Sole signal of the day, from upper-GI: RTOG 0848's negative primary (chemoRT adds nothing to adjuvant gemcitabine on OS, HR 0.96) masks a node-negative subgroup where 5-yr OS nearly doubled (28.6%→48.1%, interaction p=0.022). Margin-positive pts saw no gain; hypothesis-generating, needs FOLFIRINOX-era confirmation.

## 🍽️ GI Upper

Adjuvant pancreatic chemoRT stays unproven overall; the live RT question is whether node-negative pts are the subgroup that benefits.

### NRG Oncology/RTOG 0848 · [[NCT01013649]]

> Adjuvant chemoRT did not improve OS overall (HR 0.96, p=0.38); node-negative subgroup 5-yr OS 28.6%→48.1% (interaction p=0.022).

> [!info] Why it matters · Radiation oncology
> The RT read is the node-negative subgroup (n=91), not the null primary: 5-yr OS 28.6% vs 48.1% with CXRT (interaction p=0.022), median 3.0 vs 3.9 yr. Omit adjuvant CXRT broadly, but node-negative resected pancreatic head is the population for a confirmatory RT trial. CXRT dose and target not in source text.

- 📊 1° EP OS not improved by adding CXRT: median 2.3 vs 2.6 yr (Chemo+CXRT vs Chemo)
- 📐 OS HR 0.96 (90% CI 0.79-1.18), p=0.38 (1-sided log-rank)
- 📊 5-yr OS by pathologic N stage (Chemo vs Chemo+CXRT)

  | N stage | Chemo 5-yr OS | Chemo+CXRT 5-yr OS |
  |---|---|---|
  | N0 (55/91 events) | 28.6% (14.9-42.2) | 48.1% (33.3-62.9) |
  | N1 (215/263 events) | 21.2% (13.8-28.5) | 20.9% (13.8-28.0) |

- 📐 N-stage interaction p=0.022, the only significant treatment interaction across tested subgroups
- 📊 Node-negative subgroup medians (n=91)

  | Endpoint | Chemo (n=42) | Chemo+CXRT (n=49) |
  |---|---|---|
  | Median OS | 3.0 yr (2.2-4.0) | 3.9 yr (2.5-NR) |
  | Median DFS | 1.5 yr (0.8-2.7) | 2.3 yr (1.4-NR) |

- 🔍 RT randomization of RTOG 0848: N=354 randomized after gemcitabine-based adjuvant chemo (174 Chemo, 180 Chemo+CXRT)
- 🔍 CXRT = fluoropyrimidine-sensitized chemoradiotherapy; dose, fractionation, target volume not reported in source
- 🔗 Margin-positive pts (n=59) showed no CXRT gain: 5-yr OS 17.3%→14.3%, counter to RT-for-positive-margin rationale
- ⚠️ Gemcitabine-era backbone; node-negative signal untested vs modern FOLFIRINOX, needs independent confirmation
