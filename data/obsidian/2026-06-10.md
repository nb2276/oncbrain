# 2026-06-10

**NRG/RTOG 0848: adjuvant chemoRT null for OS overall (HR 0.96, p=0.38); only signal is an exploratory node-negative subgroup (5yr OS 48.1% vs 28.6%).**

> [!summary] TL;DR
> Adjuvant chemoRT after resected pancreatic-head cancer gives no overall OS benefit, reinforcing chemo-alone practice (ESPAC-1, CONKO-001). The lone positive read is an exploratory node-negative subgroup (5yr OS 48.1% vs 28.6%, P-interaction 0.022), hypothesis-generating and gemcitabine-era. Not a basis to add RT.

### NRG Oncology/RTOG 0848 · [[NCT01013649]]

> No OS benefit from adjuvant CXRT overall (HR 0.96, p=0.38); node-negative subgroup 5yr OS 48.1% vs 28.6% favoring CXRT.

> [!info] Why it matters · Radiation oncology
> The RT read lives in the node-negative subgroup: 5yr OS 48.1% vs 28.6% and median OS 3.9 vs 3.0 yr favoring CXRT (nodal P-interaction 0.022), against a null overall HR 0.96. Dose, fractionation, and target volume are not in source, so transferability is unverified. It reopens selective CXRT in node-negative resected disease, not routine addition.

- 📊 1° EP OS null overall: HR 0.96 (90% CI 0.79-1.18), p=0.38 (1-sided log-rank)
- 📐 Median OS 2.3 vs 2.6 yr (Chemo+CXRT vs Chemo); adding RT numerically favors chemo alone
- 📊 5yr OS estimate by nodal status (2-sided 95% CI)

  | Nodal status | Chemo | Chemo+CXRT |
  |---|---|---|
  | N0 (n=91) | 28.6% (14.9-42.2) | 48.1% (33.3-62.9) |
  | N1 (n=263) | 21.2% (13.8-28.5) | 20.9% (13.8-28.0) |

- 📊 Node-negative subgroup medians (yr, 95% CI)

  | Endpoint (N0) | Chemo | Chemo+CXRT |
  |---|---|---|
  | Median OS | 3.0 (2.2-4.0) | 3.9 (2.5-NR) |
  | Median DFS | 1.5 (0.8-2.7) | 2.3 (1.4-NR) |

- 🔍 Phase III RT randomization; N=354, resected pancreatic head adenoca after gemcitabine-based adjuvant chemo, ± fluoropyrimidine-sensitized CXRT
- 🔍 RT dose, fractionation, and target volume not reported in source text
- ⚠️ Node-negative OS benefit is an exploratory subgroup (nodal P-interaction 0.022); hypothesis-generating, needs independent confirmation
- ⚠️ Gemcitabine-era backbone (gem alone 236, gem+erlotinib 100); predates adjuvant mFOLFIRINOX standard
- 🔗 Reinforces adjuvant chemo-alone practice (ESPAC-1, CONKO-001); no routine role for adjuvant chemoRT
