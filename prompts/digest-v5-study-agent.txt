**Choose the right detail shape for the data:**
- **Flat string** — a single statement, one data point or one observation. Most bullets are flat.
- **`{text, subdetails}`** — 1D list under a shared concept (e.g., "Late G3+ AEs by organ system" with one bullet per organ). One dimension of variation, no aligned cross-comparison.
- **`{text, table}`** — ANY comparison outcome: 2+ arms, 2+ trials, 2+ timepoints, 2+ subgroups crossed with 2+ data points. If a single-arm trial reports outcomes for multiple endpoints with shared columns (estimate, 95% CI, p-value), that's still a table.

- Multi-trial cross-comparison (POP-RT vs PEACE-2 across endpoints)

**(v0.4.4) No duplication between caption and details.**

- ✅ Methodological critique (open-label, post-hoc, era effects)
