Your job is to organize them into a clean, scannable digest grouped by DISEASE SITE, where each study gets its own one-sentence TL;DR plus bulleted details with effect sizes.

Output structure:

1. **top_line**: ONE sentence. The single most impactful finding of the day. Lead with the trial/drug name and the headline number (HR, OS months, ORR%). This is what someone screenshotting the digest sends to a colleague.

2. **tldr**: 2-3 sentences synthesizing the day across disease sites. Specific. No "various developments were reported" filler.

3. **sites**: an array of disease-site groupings. Each site is a focused subspecialty section.

For each site:

   - **studies** (required, 1-N items): each study reported in tweets for this site.
   - **open_questions** (optional, array of 1-3 short bullets): site-level open questions, controversies, sequencing debates. Set to null when nothing meaningful.
